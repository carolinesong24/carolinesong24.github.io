    Carrie's Winter Wonderland
    Internship, 8 weeks

    We partnered with Carrie Underwood and Universal Music to create a Winter Wonderland for her fans with exclusive content and surprises.

    In celebration of Carrie Underwood’s first Holiday album ‘My Wish’, the digital activation included personal messages from Carrie, exclusive BTS content, gamified giveaways, and access to holiday merchandise.

    Tools:
    Figma
    Adobe AfterEffects
    Adobe Illustrator
    Adobe Photoshop
    Role:
    UI Prototyping
    Motion Graphics
    Branding
    Conceptual Design
    Team:
    TH Experiential

    Final Interactive Website –

    We designed Carrie’s Winter Wonderland with the intent to bring Carrie’s album artwork to life in the digital space.

    Through BTS footage of Carrie’s new album, personal recipes and workout routines, gamified experiences, merchandise, and a donation page, each part of this virtual event was created to bring the magic of the holidays to every fans’ screen for the two months it ran.


    Examples of Key Interaction Pages

    Social Media Content —

    In order to promote the event and drive traffic to the event, our team designed strategy and created a series of animations to be posted on Carrie Underwood’s official social channels.


    Curated Social Media Posts

    In just few days, the posts got over 200K views, driving thousands to the site to explore her curated content on how to best enjoy the holiday season.


    Live Posts from Left to Right: Instagram, Facebook, Twitter

    Final Thoughts —

    To create an event for a musical artist that I have listened to growing up was a dream come true. I was able to develop my skills in understanding a client’s needs and expanding on them to design a immersive experience for them. It was truly amazing to see my work come to life on a live site, and create content that is posted on high-profile channels in real-time.

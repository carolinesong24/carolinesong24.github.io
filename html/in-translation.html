    In Translation
    Fall 2020, 3 weeks

    The Korean language is a beautiful one. But like any other language, it has its ridiculous moments, especially through its idioms. In this project, I took five common Korean expressions and turned them into illustrative posters which depict my intepretation of their direct meanings.

    I started this independent project in hopes of allowing viewers to take joy in these simple expressions and celebrate the beauty of the Korean language. Enjoy this absurd and colorful system of illustrations!

    This project is currently in process!

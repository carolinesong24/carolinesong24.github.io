    communications designer @ carnegie mellon

    I’m an interaction designer focused on creating experiences that are delightful and to help people create lasting memories with one another. I aim to find ways to bridge people together through art, writing and visual design. Above all else, I believe every design should be made with clear intent, and be intuitive, informative, and beautiful.

    Interested in...

    🔆Brand Direction

    Previously jumpstarted the branding process and visual identity at CyLab initiative, the Internet of Things.

    💻Web Design

    Always practicing my front-end development skills, I love the process of designing and making a website come alive piece by piece!

    🔶Animation

    Animating through hand drawings and stop motion brings a human touch into designs that cannot be replicated by a computer.

    🖌Illustration

    I find illustrating to be one of the most versatile and easiest ways to communicate my ideas.
